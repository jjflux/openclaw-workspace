## SOCIAL DM AUTOMATION

### Can You Automate DMs? Sort Of.

**Facebook DMs:**
- **Manual is safest.** Facebook aggressively detects automation and will ban accounts.
- **Semi-automated approach:** Use a VA or set aside 30 min/day to send 10-15 DMs from the scripts we have
- Tools like Dripify (LinkedIn) exist but nothing reliable for FB DMs
- **Risk:** Account ban, marketplace restrictions

**Instagram DMs:**
- Similar to Facebook ‚Äî automation = risk of ban
- **ManyChat** can automate replies to people who DM YOU (inbound), but not cold outbound
- Manual outreach: 10-15 DMs/day is sustainable without getting flagged

**LinkedIn:**
- **Dripify** ($59/mo) or **Expandi** ($99/mo) ‚Äî automate connection requests + follow-up messages
- Less relevant for roofers (most aren't active on LinkedIn) but worth testing
- Can send 20-30 connection requests/day safely

**The honest take:** Social DMs should be MANUAL for now. It's more personal, lower risk, and roofers can tell when something is automated. Spend 30 min/day on this.
