7. SMS/Text Message Communications

Consent to Receive SMS Messages: By providing your mobile phone number and checking the SMS consent box, you expressly consent to receive text messages from Land More Calls at the number provided. You must opt in to receive any SMS communications from us. Opting in to SMS is not a condition of purchasing any property, goods, or services.

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies and depends on your account activity and the services you've subscribed to. You may receive appointment confirmations, service updates, and account-related notifications.

Standard Rates Apply: Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out Instructions: You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message from us
  • Contacting us directly at jason@landmorecalls.com
  • Updating your preferences in your account settings

Help: For assistance with text messages, reply HELP to any message or contact us at jason@landmorecalls.com.

Consent Withdrawal: You may withdraw your consent at any time using any of the opt-out methods listed above.

Default Setting: The SMS consent checkbox is unchecked by default. You must actively check the box to receive text messages.
