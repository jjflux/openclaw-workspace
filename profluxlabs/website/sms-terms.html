7. SMS/Text Message Communications

Consent to Receive SMS Messages: By providing your mobile phone number and checking the SMS consent box (if applicable), you consent to receive text messages from ProFluxLabs at the number provided. This consent is optional and not required to use our services.

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies and depends on your account activity and the services you've subscribed to. You may receive appointment confirmations, service updates, and promotional messages.

Standard Rates Apply: Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out Instructions: You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message from us
  • Contacting us directly at jason@profluxlabs.com
  • Updating your preferences in your account settings

Help: For assistance with text messages, reply HELP to any message or contact us at jason@profluxlabs.com.

Consent Withdrawal: You understand that you are not required to consent to receiving text messages as a condition of purchasing any property, goods, or services from ProFluxLabs.

Default Setting: The SMS consent checkbox is unchecked by default. You must actively choose to receive text messages.
