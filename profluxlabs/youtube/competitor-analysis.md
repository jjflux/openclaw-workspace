## Key Patterns Across Successful Channels

### What Gets Views
1. **"How to" tutorials** — Always searchable, always relevant
2. **Income/results reveals** — "I made $X doing Y" drives curiosity
3. **Raw experiments** — "I tried doing X, here's what happened"
4. **Tool reviews/comparisons** — People search for these before buying
5. **Step-by-step systems** — "The complete system for [outcome]"

### Title Patterns That Work
- Numbers: "7 Ways...", "$10K/mo...", "in 30 Days..."
- Results: "(RAW RESULTS)", "Here's What Happened"
- Curiosity: "...And This Changed Everything", "Nobody Talks About This"
- Specificity: "for Roofers", "Using AI", "in 2026"

### Thumbnail Patterns
- **Face + emotion** (80%+ of top videos have a face)
- **Bold, sans-serif text** (3-5 words max)
- **High contrast** (dark background + bright text OR bright background + dark text)
- **Screenshots/proof** (dashboards, results, money)
- **Before/After** split layouts

### Posting Frequency
- Most successful channels: 2-3 videos/week
- Shorts: Daily or near-daily
- Long-form: 1-2x/week

### Monetization Stack (most common)
1. Courses/Programs ($497-2,000+)
2. Community (Skool/Circle — $30-99/mo)
3. Consulting/Done-for-you services
4. Affiliate links
5. Sponsorships (after 10K+ subs)
6. AdSense (smallest but nice passive income)
