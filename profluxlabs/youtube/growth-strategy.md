## 4. YouTube Shorts Strategy

### Why Shorts Are Critical
- YouTube aggressively promotes Shorts (competing with TikTok)
- 50M+ daily Shorts viewers
- 2.5x more engagement than long-form
- Separate feed = double exposure
- Jason already has proven viral TikTok content to repurpose

### Shorts Best Practices
1. **Hook in first 1-2 seconds** — "This AI tool just booked 3 roofing jobs in one day"
2. **Film in 9:16 vertical** — Always high quality, good lighting
3. **Add on-screen text/captions** — Most watch without sound
4. **Keep 30-45 seconds** (sweet spot for algorithm)
5. **End with a hook** — "Follow for Part 2" or "Comment if you want the tutorial"
6. **Post at peak times:** 12pm-3pm EST and 6pm-9pm EST

### Shorts Content Ideas
- "I automated this roofer's entire follow-up system" (screen recording)
- "POV: You're a roofer losing leads because you don't respond in 5 minutes" (skit)
- Quick AI tool demos (30 seconds showing before/after)
- Soccer training + "This is why I grind at 5am" motivation
- Client result reveals: "This roofer went from 2 to 15 leads/week"

### TikTok → YouTube Shorts Repurposing
- Remove TikTok watermark before uploading (YouTube penalizes watermarked content)
- Adjust captions/text for YouTube formatting
- Re-edit hooks for YouTube audience (slightly different than TikTok)
- Don't just repost — optimize title, description, hashtags for YouTube
- Best performing TikToks should be re-filmed for YouTube (higher quality)

- "AI for small business contractors"

### SEO Checklist for Every Video
1. **Title:** Primary keyword in first 5 words, under 60 characters
2. **Description:** 
   - First 2 lines: keyword-rich summary (shows in search)
   - Full description: 200-500 words with natural keyword usage
   - Include timestamps/chapters
   - Links to resources, social media, website
3. **Tags:** 5-10 relevant tags (primary keyword + variations + competitor names)
4. **Hashtags:** 3-5 in description (#AIAutomation #RoofingBusiness #Shorts for Shorts)
5. **Closed Captions:** Upload custom transcript (helps YouTube understand content)
6. **Chapters:** Add timestamps for key sections (boosts engagement signals)
7. **Cards & End Screens:** Link to related videos and playlists
8. **Custom Thumbnail:** Designed before filming (more on this below)

### Tools
- **VidIQ or TubeBuddy:** Keyword research, competitor tracking, SEO scoring
- **Google Trends:** Validate topic interest
- **YouTube Search Suggest:** Type partial queries to find what people search

## 7. Thumbnail & Title Optimization

### Thumbnail Rules
1. **3-element max:** Face/object + text + background
2. **Bold, contrasting colors** (yellow, red, white on dark backgrounds)
3. **Large, readable text** (max 4-5 words)
4. **Expressive face** — surprise, excitement, concern
5. **Before/After format** works great for AI automation demos
6. **Consistent branding** — same font, color palette across videos
7. **Test at small size** — thumbnails must be readable as a tiny square

### Title Formulas That Work
- **Number + Benefit:** "7 AI Tools That Book Roofing Jobs on Autopilot"
- **How-To:** "How to Automate Your Roofing Business (Step-by-Step)"
- **Result + Method:** "47 Leads in 30 Days Using This AI System"
- **Challenge/Experiment:** "I Automated a Roofer's Business in 48 Hours"
- **Curiosity Gap:** "This AI Trick is Replacing Roofing Sales Teams"
- **Controversy:** "Why 90% of Roofers Are Wasting Money on Marketing"

### A/B Testing
- YouTube now offers thumbnail A/B testing — USE IT
- Test 2-3 thumbnails per video
- Track CTR in YouTube Studio (target: 6-8%+ for your niche)

## 8. Content Repurposing Strategy

### TikTok → YouTube Pipeline
1. Create TikTok content (fast, casual, trend-driven)
2. Identify top performers (>1K views or high engagement)
3. Re-film the concept for YouTube Shorts (better quality, no watermark)
4. Expand the best Shorts into long-form tutorials
5. Extract quotes/clips from long-form → new Shorts

### The Content Multiplication System
**1 Long-form video (10-15 min) → **
- 3-5 YouTube Shorts (key moments with captions)
- 3-5 TikToks (re-edited for TikTok format)
- 3-5 Instagram Reels
- 1 LinkedIn article or post
- 1 Twitter/X thread
- Blog post for SEO (embed the YouTube video)
- Email newsletter content

### Tools for Repurposing
- **Opus Clip or Vizard:** AI auto-clips long videos into Shorts
- **Descript:** Edit videos like a document, auto-captions
- **Canva:** Quick thumbnail creation
- **CapCut:** TikTok-style editing with trending effects
