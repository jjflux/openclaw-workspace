3. **11:00am** ‚Äî Film long-form video(s)
4. **1:00pm** ‚Äî Film B-roll, lifestyle clips, cutaways

- **Mon-Tue:** Edit Shorts for the week (quick ‚Äî 15 min each)
- **Tue-Wed:** Edit long-form video (1-2 hours)

- **Thu:** Schedule all uploads, write titles/descriptions/tags
